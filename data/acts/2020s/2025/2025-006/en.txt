Foreign Loans (Repeal) Act, No. 6 of 2025 
1
L.D.-O. 80/2024
AN  ACT TO REPEAL THE FOREIGN LOANS 
ACT, NO. 29 OF 1957
BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist 
Republic of Sri Lanka as follows:-
1. This Act may be cited as the Foreign Loans (Repeal) 
Act, No. 6 of 2025.
2. The Foreign Loans Act, No. 29 of 1957 is hereby 
repealed.
3. Notwithstanding the repeal of the Foreign Loans 
Act, No. 29 of 1957 (hereinafter referred to as the “Foreign 
Loans Act”) –
 
(a) every agreement relating to a foreign loan, a 
guarantee relating to a foreign loan and any 
contract, bond, promissory note or other document 
required by such agreement or guarantee signed 
under section 2 of the Foreign Loans Act and 
in force on the day immediately preceding the 
date of commencement of this Act shall, in terms 
of section 38 of the Public Debt Management 
Act, No. 33 of 2024, be deemed, on and after the 
date of commencement of this Act, to have been 
validly signed under the Public Debt Management 
Act; and
 
(b) every order made to give effect to an agreement or 
guarantee referred to in paragraph (a), prior to the 
date of commencement of this Act under section 4 
of the Foreign Loans Act, and  in force on the day

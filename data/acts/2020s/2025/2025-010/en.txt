BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka as follows: -

1. (1) This Act may be cited as the Budgetary Relief Allowance of Workers (Amendment) Act, No.10 of 2025.

Short title and date of operation

(2) The provisions of this Act shall be deemed to have come into operation on April 1, 2025.

2. Section 3 of the Budgetary Relief Allowance of Workers Act, No. 4 of 2016 is hereby amended as follows: -

Amendment of section 3 of Act, No. 4 of 2016

(1) in subsection (1) thereof, by the substitution for the words and gures "With e ect from May 1, 2015,", of the words and gures "For the period commencing on May 1, 2015 and ending on March 31, 2025, ''; and

(2) in subsection (2) thereof, by the substitution for the words and gures "commencing from January 1, 2016", of the words and gures "for the period commencing on January 1, 2016 and ending on March 31, 2025,".

3. In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.

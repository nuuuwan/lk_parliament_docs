[Certified on 17th of February, 2015]

L.D.- O 76/2024

AN ACT TO PROVIDE FOR THE CALLING OF FRESH

NOMINATIONS AND THE HOLDING OF ELECTIONS IN RESPECT OF CERTAIN LOCAL AUTHORITIES WHERE THE ELECTIONS OF SUCH LOCAL AUTHORITIES HAVE BEEN CALLED AND POSTPONED; AND TO PROVIDE FOR MATTERS CONNECTED THEREWITH OF INCIDENTAL THERETO. WHEREAS notices of nominations were published under section 26 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262), that nomination papers will be received in respect of elections to the Local Authorities set out in the Schedule hereto: AND WHEREAS March 9, 2023 was fixed for the holding of elections for the aforesaid Local Authorities : AND WHEREAS the said elections were not conducted on such date: AND WHEREAS the submission of nominations for the aforesaid local authorities was concluded in January 2023 and it has become necessary to provide for those who have registered in the register of electors in the year 2024 as new voters, who would otherwise be deprived of their right to vote at the new date of polls fixed and their right to submit nomination papers for the elections for the aforesaid Local Authorities set out in the Schedule hereto: AND WHEREAS the local Authorities Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 30 of 2023 has come into operation on November 17, 2023 and it has become necessary to provide for the nominations by the youth candidates in accordance with the provisions relating to the nomination of youth candidates under section 28 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance, (Chapter 262), when holding fresh elections in respect of such Local Authorities set out in the Schedule hereto:

Preamble

AND WHEREAS due to the period of time that has elapsed since the submission of nominations in respect of such Local Authorities set out in the Schedule hereto, it has become necessary to provide for the calling of fresh nominations and to make provisions for the holding of fresh elections in respect of such Local Authorities set out in the Schedule hereto: NOW THEREFORE, BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka as follows :-

1. This Act may be cited as the Local Authorities Elections (Special Provisions) Act, No. 1 of 2025. 2. (1) Nomination papers submitted for the Local Authorities elections due to be held on March 9, 2023 under section 28 of the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262) (hereinafter referred to as the Ordinance) in respect of the Local Authorities set out in the Schedule hereto in response to the notices of nominations published under section 26 of the Ordinance are hereby deemed to be of no force and effect as if such nomination papers had never been submitted.

Short title

Provisions relating to nominations, notices and deposits in respect of elections to certain Local Authorities

(2) The notices published under subsection (1) of section 38 of the Ordinance, in respect of elections to the Local Authorities set out in the Schedule hereto are hereby revoked.

(3) Deposits made under section 29 of the Ordinance in respect of candidates nominated by any recognized political party or an independent group for elections to the Local Authorities set out in the Schedule hereto, shall notwithstanding anything to the contrary in section 30 of the Ordinance, be refunded to the person by whom the deposit was made, on the production by such person, of the receipt issued to such person under subsection (3) of section 29 of the Ordinance.

Act, N0. 1 0f 2025

Steps to be commenced for holding of elections

3. Steps shall be commenced under the provisions of the Ordinance for the holding of elections to the Local Authorities set out in the Schedule hereto:

Provided that, the notices of nominations in terms of section 26 of the Ordinance shall be published on such date as the Election Commission may determine by Order published in the Gazette and such date shall however, be a date not later than three months from the date of coming into operation of this Act.

4. In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.

Sinhala text to prevail in case of inconsistency

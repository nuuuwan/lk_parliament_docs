AN ACT TO AMEND THE CODE OF CRIMINAL PROCEDURE
ACT, NO. 15 OF 1979
BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka as follows: -
1. This Act may be cited as the Code of Criminal
Procedure (Amendment) Act, No. 38 of 2022.
2. Section 281 of the Code of Criminal Procedure Act,
No. 15 of 1979 (hereinafter referred to as the “principal
enactment”) is hereby amended by the substitution for the
words, “appears to the court to be under the age of eighteen
years,” of the words “is under the age of eighteen years,
at the time of the commission of such offence by such
person,”.
3. Section 286 of the principal enactment is hereby
amended by the substitution for the words and figures, “In
this section and in section 288 the expression” of the words
“In this section the expression”.
4. Section 288 of the principal enactment is hereby
repealed and the following section is substituted therefor:–
288. When any person has been sentenced
under section 53 of the Penal Code
(Chapter 19), such person shall be detained in
an institution established under any written
law for the detention of persons under the age
of eighteen years, for a period specified in the
sentence and subject to the provisions of such
written law.”.

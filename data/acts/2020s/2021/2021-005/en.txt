AN ACT TO AMEND THE PENAL CODE ( CHAPTER 19 )

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka as follows:-

1. This Act may be cited as the Penal Code (Amendment) Act, No. 5 of 2021.

Short title

Replacement of the word Sri Lanka for the word Ceylon in Chapter 19

2. The word Ceylon, wherever it appears in the Penal Code (Chapter 19) (hereinafter referred to as the principal enactment) other than in sections 3, 18A, 120, 138, 158, 159, 160, 225 and 256A thereof, shall be substituted with the word Sri Lanka.

Replacement of the word Republic for the word Queen in the principal enactment

3. The word Queen, wherever it appears in the principal enactment, other than in sections 19, 115 and 120 thereof, shall be substituted with the word Republic.

4. Section 12 of the principal enactment is hereby repealed and the following section is substituted therefor:-

Replacement of section 12 of the principal enactment Republic

12. The word Republic denotes the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka..

5. Section 13 of the principal enactment is hereby repealed.

Repeal of section 13 of the principal enactment Replacement of section 14 of the principal enactment Government

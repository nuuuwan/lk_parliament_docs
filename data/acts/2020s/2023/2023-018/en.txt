L.D.—O. (Inc.) 4/2019
AN  ACT TO AMEND THE CHARTERED INSTITUTE OF TRANSPORT OF
SRI LANKA (INCORPORATION) ACT, NO. 8 OF 2000
BE it  enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka as follows :—
1.
This Act may be cited as the Chartered Institute of
Transport of Sri Lanka (Incorporation) (Amendment) Act,
No. 18 of  2023.
2.
The long title to the Chartered Institute of Transport
of Sri Lanka (Incorporation) Act, No. 8 of 2000, (hereinafter
referred to as the “principal enactment”) is hereby amended
by the substitution, for the words “Chartered Institute of
Transport of Sri Lanka.” of the words “Chartered Institute of
Logistics and Transport of Sri Lanka.”.
  3.
(1) In the principal enactment and in any other written
law there shall be substituted for the words “Chartered
Institute of  Transport of Sri Lanka” wherever such words
appear in the principal enactment and such other written law
of the words “Chartered Institute of Logistics and Transport
of Sri Lanka”.
(2) Every reference to the “Chartered Institute of  Transport
of Sri Lanka” in any notice, notification, contract,
communication or other document shall be read and
construed as a reference to the “Chartered Institute of Logistics
and Transport of Sri Lanka”.
4.
For the avoidance of doubts it is hereby declared
that the “Chartered Institute of Logistics and Transport of Sri
Lanka” shall for all purposes be deemed to be the successor
to the “Chartered Institute of Transport of Sri Lanka”.
